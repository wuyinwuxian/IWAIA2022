Bei Sun received his Ph.D. in Control Science and Engineering from Central South University, China in 2015, and was with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Polytechnic School of Engineering, New York University, United States from 2012 to 2014. He is currently an Associate Professor of Central South University. He is a member of IFAC TC6.2. His research interests include data-driven modeling, optimization and control of nonferrous metallurgical processes.
