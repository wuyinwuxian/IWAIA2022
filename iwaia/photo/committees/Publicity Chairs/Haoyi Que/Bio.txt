Haoyi Que received a Bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and got his Ph.D. from Zhejiang University in 2018. Now, he is employed as a high-level talent by Shenzhen Polytechnic. Currently, he presided over a National Natural Science Foundation, participated in a number of national key research and development plans, key special projects, and other provincial and ministerial projects. At the same time, he published more than ten SCI journal papers including the IEEE Transactions series and obtained multiple patents. Also, he is a reviewer of several well-known journals in the industry. The main research field includes nonlinear adaptive control theory and application, restricted networked control, sampling control, and artificial intelligence.
