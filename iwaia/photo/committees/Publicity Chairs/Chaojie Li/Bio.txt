Chaojie Li received the B.Eng. degree in electronic science and technology and the M.Eng. degree in computer science from Chongqing University, Chongqing, China, in 2007 and 2011, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree from RMIT University, Melbourne, Australia, in 2017, where he was a Research Fellow for one and a half years. He is a Senior Algorithm Engineer with Alibaba Group. His current research interests include graph representation learning, distributed optimization and control in smart grid, neural networks, and their application.
