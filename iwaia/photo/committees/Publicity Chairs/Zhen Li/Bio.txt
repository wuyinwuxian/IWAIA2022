Zhen Li received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in fundamental mathematics from Yangzhou University, Jiangsu, China, in 2009 and 2013, and the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from Donghua University, Shanghai, China, in 2016, respectively.

He is currently a Lecturer with the School of Automation, Xi-an University of Posts & Telecommunications, China. From 2015 to 2016, he was a Program Aid with Texas A & M University at Qatar for half a year. In July 2016, he was a Post-Doctoral Research Associate with Texas A & M University at Qatar for one month. His current research interests include hybrid dynamical systems, multi-agent systems, and distributed control systems.
