Xin Wang, associate professor, master supervisor. Research fields include: multi-agent systems, impulse control, neural networks and other related fields. Presided over 1 National Natural Science Foundation of China, 2 Chongqing Natural Science Foundation of China, 1 General Project of China Postdoctoral Fund, etc. He has published more than 10 academic papers in international journals including IEEE Transactions on Systems Man and Cybernetics: Systems, IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering, Nueral Networks, etc. included in SCI. Served as a reviewer for many international journals, such as IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TSMC-A.
